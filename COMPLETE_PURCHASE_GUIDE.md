## Step-by-Step to Complete Your Order:

### Step 1: Enter Delivery Address ✍️
Look for the "Delivery Address" section and type your address:
```
Example: 123 Main Street, Valasaravakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600001
```

### Step 2: Payment Method Already Selected ✅
- **State Bank of India** is already selected
- Account ending in **7980**
- Balance: **₹50000.00** (more than enough!)

### Step 3: Click "Pay ₹345" Button 💰
- The big green button at the bottom
- This will start the payment process

### Step 4: Set Payment PIN (First Time Only) 🔐
A dialog will pop up asking you to create a PIN:
1. Enter any **4 digits** (example: 1234)
2. Confirm the same **4 digits**
3. Click **"Set PIN"**
4. You'll see: "✅ Payment PIN set successfully"

### Step 5: Verify Your PIN ✓
Another dialog will appear:
1. Enter the **same 4 digits** you just set
2. Click **"Verify"**

### Step 6: Payment Success! 🎊
- Order will be created automatically
- Your cart will be cleared
- You'll see the success screen
- Balance will be deducted: ₹50000 - ₹345 = ₹49655

## Common Errors & Quick Fixes:

### ❌ "Please enter delivery address"
**Fix:** Scroll up and fill in the delivery address field

### ❌ "Please select a bank account"
**Fix:** The account should be auto-selected. If not, tap on the State Bank of India option

### ❌ "Insufficient balance"
**Fix:** Won't happen - you have ₹50000 and order is only ₹345

### ❌ "Incorrect PIN"
**Fix:** Make sure you enter the exact same 4 digits you set earlier
