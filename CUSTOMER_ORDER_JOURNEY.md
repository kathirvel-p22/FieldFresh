## Alternative Ways to Order

### Method 1: From Farmers Tab 👨‍🌾
1. Go to "Farmers" tab (2nd icon)
2. Browse nearby farmers
3. Click on a farmer
4. See all their products
5. Add to cart
6. Checkout

### Method 2: Quick Order (Order Now Button) ⚡
1. Browse products in Market
2. Click on product
3. Adjust quantity
4. Click "₹XXX Order Now" button
5. Goes directly to checkout
6. Complete payment

### Method 3: Group Buy 👥
1. Go to "Group Buy" tab (4th icon)
2. See group buying opportunities
3. Join existing group
4. Get bulk discounts
5. Checkout together

## Summary: Complete Customer Journey

```
Browse Products → Select Product → Add to Cart → View Cart → 
Checkout → Enter Address → Select Payment → Enter PIN → 
Payment Success → Track Order
```

**Time to Complete**: 2-3 minutes
**Steps**: 9 easy steps
**Result**: Fresh produce delivered to your door! 🎉

## What Makes It Easy

✅ **Visual Product Cards** - See photos, prices, ratings
✅ **Freshness Scores** - Know how fresh the produce is
✅ **Distance Display** - See how far the farmer is
✅ **Cart Badge** - Always know how many items in cart
✅ **Flexible Checkout** - Optional address, auto-selected payment
✅ **Secure PIN** - 4-digit PIN for payment security
✅ **Real-time Tracking** - See order status updates
✅ **Platform Fees Shown** - Transparent pricing

**The customer has full control to choose any products they want!** 🛒
